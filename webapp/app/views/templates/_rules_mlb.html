MLB Scoring Scoring

For Pitchers:
  • 10 pts per Strike Out
  • -10 pts per Earned Run
  • 10 pts per Inning Pitched
  • 40 pts per Home run
  • 40 pts per Win
  • -5 for for a hit or walk or hbp (hit by pitch)
For Hitters:
  • 10 pts per Single
  • 20 pts per Double
  • 30 pts per Triple
  • 40 pts per Home run
  • 10 pts per Walk
  • 10 pts per Hit By Pitch
  • 10 pts per Run
  • 10 pts per Run Batten In
  • 20 pts per Stolen Base
  • -2.5 pts per Out
